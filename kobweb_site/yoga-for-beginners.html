Yoga for Beginners 🧘‍♀️

This Yoga for Beginners routine is ideal for those who are new to yoga or looking to refresh their practice. It incorporates fundamental poses and breathing techniques to help you stretch, relax, and develop a connection between mind and body.

💫 Warm-Up (5 minutes)

  • Deep breathing (inhale for 4 counts, exhale for 4 counts)
  • Gentle neck stretches (side to side, up and down)
  • Wrist and ankle rotations

🌟 Yoga Poses Routine

1. Mountain Pose (Tadasana) (Hold for 30 seconds) 🏔️

How to do it:

  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart and arms by your sides.
  2. Engage your legs and lift through the crown of your head.
  3. Take deep breaths and feel rooted to the ground.

Tip: Keep your shoulders relaxed and avoid locking your knees.

2. Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana) (Hold for 30 seconds)

How to do it:

  1. Start in a tabletop position (hands and knees on the mat).
  2. Tuck your toes and lift your hips up and back, forming an upside-down "V."
  3. Keep your hands shoulder-width apart and feet hip-width apart.
  4. Press your heels toward the floor, and relax your head between your arms.
Tip

Focus on elongating your spine and engaging your core.

3. Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I) (Hold for 30 seconds per side) 🦸‍♀️

How to do it:

  1. Step your left foot forward and bend your knee at a 90-degree angle.
  2. Extend your right leg straight behind you and keep your hips square.
  3. Reach your arms overhead, palms facing each other.
  4. Keep your chest open and shoulders relaxed.

Tip: Press through the back heel for stability.

4. Child’s Pose (Balasana) (Hold for 1 minute)

  • Kneel on the mat and sit back on your heels.
  • Lower your forehead to the floor and stretch your arms forward or by your sides.
  • Breathe deeply and relax into the pose.

🔄 Cool-Down (5 minutes)

  • Seated forward bend (Paschimottanasana) for hamstring stretch.
  • Supine twist to release tension in the spine.
  • Savasana (Corpse Pose) for deep relaxation.

📅 Suggested Weekly Schedule

  • Tuesday: Yoga for Beginners
  • Thursday: Gentle Stretching & Mindfulness
  • Saturday: Yoga for Beginners

Tips for Progress

  • Focus on breathwork to deepen your practice.
  • Don’t push yourself into discomfort—let flexibility come gradually.
  • Stay patient and consistent; yoga is a journey!
